# DMX

Welcome to my Ph.D.

## Subject

**Name** : Ephemeral communication backbones for data harvesting in wireless sensor networks.

**Explanation** : The goal of my Ph.D. is to propose solution for efficient data harvesting methods into wireless sensor networks without any fixed infrastructure. To achieved that I will use mobile sinks such as **data mule**, UAV (Unmaned Aerial Vehicles), UGV (Unmaned Ground Vehicle) and low orbit satellites. These methods shall take advantage of **opportunistic communications** to maximize PDR (packet delivery ratio) and minimize latency, AoI (Age of Information) and energy consumption. Technics such as **Multi-RAT** (Radio Access Technologies) and **edge computing** could be used to achieve that.
